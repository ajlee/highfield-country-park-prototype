Seventy acres of nature on the doorstep of Levenshulme and Reddish where you can relax from the stresses of life. It is a haven of biodiversity, with many species of plants, trees and birds thrive in a diverse park.

What makes Highfield Country Park unique in the area is that nature is allowed to thrive. Grass grows long, wild flowers grow of their own accord, and trees drop their seeds and produce new life as they please. As you walk, you may even forget you are in a bustling city, and think that you have gone into remote countryside.
